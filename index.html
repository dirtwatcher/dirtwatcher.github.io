Fire Emblem: Awakening

*Fire Emblem: Awakening* for the 3DS, colloquially known as FE13, was the first *Fire Emblem* game I ever played, and was single-handedly responsible for my becoming a fan of Strategy-RPGs. I had to have more of that sort of tactical gameplay after finishing it for the first time and this included checking out some of the other *Fire Emblem* games. After so many years, playing the newer games as they came out, trying out many different kinds of SRPGs, recently finishing *Shadow Dragon* and *New Mystery of the Emblem*, I decided to try *Awakening* again, curious as to what I'd think of it.

As it turned out, I still loved it, even though I think I've played tighter games, even after having seen the criticisms over the years and agreeing with some of them, and the only thing I can think of is that I find it fun. It's enjoyable, and it's not just the gameplay that factors in here, but the presentation too.

The mainline games after Awakening are

  1. Fire Emblem: Fates
  2. Fire Emblem: Echoes
  3. Fire Emblem: Three Houses
